The Viva

Versatile, Modern and Stylish.

Our most popular sofa bed! The Viva sofa bed is versatile, offering storage, comfort and dual functionality. Expertly crafted, the conversion from sofa to bed and vice-versa is smooth and simple, meaning you won't break a sweat! The Viva was crafted with ease-of-use, practicality and longevity in mind, providing you with incredible, long-lasting value.

Sofa Highlights

  • Soft Polyester Upholstery
  • Foam-filled Cushions
  • Sofa Bed Conversion
  • Solid Wood Frame
  • Spacious Storage

Spacious Storage

This sofa also has storage! No more need to pick up dust-covered throws, duvets, and cushions off the floor ... just place them in the easy-to-access storage space so your room can remain clutter-free!

Large Bed

The Viva sofa doubles as a bed - no more sleeping on the floor for your house guests, and no more worrying when a friend suddenly plans to stay the night!
